Subhamon Supantha

Pre-doctoral Researcher at IIT Bombay

I am currently a pre-doctoral researcher at the Center for Machine Intelligence and Data Science (CMInDS), IIT Bombay, working under the mentorship of Prof. Parthe Pandit. My broader goal is to contribute to the theoretical foundations of modern machine learning, particularly in understanding learning dynamics and decision-making processes.

My research interests lie in Deep Learning Theory and Sequential Decision Making. I aim to understand the foundations of modern machine learning and decision processes.

Research Interests

Deep Learning Theory, Neural Tangent Kernels, Sequential Decision Making, Online Convex Optimization, Bandits.
