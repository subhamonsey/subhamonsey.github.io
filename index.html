Subhamon Supantha

Pre-doctoral Researcher at IIT Bombay

I am currently a pre-doctoral researcher at the Centre for Machine Intelligence and Data Science (CMInDS), IIT Bombay, working with Prof. Parthe Pandit.

Before this, I did my master's from the Chennai Mathematical Institute (CMI), where I was supervised by Prof. K. V. Subrahmanyam. During my time at CMI, I visited Tata Institute of Fundamental Research (TIFR), Mumbai as a summer project researcher, where I worked with Prof. Abhishek Sinha.

My research interests lie in Deep Learning Theory and Sequential Decision Making. I aim to understand the foundations of modern machine learning and decision processes.

Research Interests

Deep Learning Theory, Sequential Decision Making, Online Learning, Bandits, Kernels, Game Theory
